📜 MysticTwinSMP Guidelines

1. Be Kind to Others: While griefing is part of the game, keep chat respectful and avoid toxic or offensive language. We want a positive community!

2. Griefing and Raiding are Allowed: Raiding bases, looting, and griefing are part of the survival challenge. However, do not engage in harassment or target the same players repeatedly.

3. No Hacking or Cheating: Any form of hacking, including x-ray mods, fly hacks, or autoclickers, is strictly prohibited. This includes using external tools to give yourself an unfair advantage.

4. No Duplication Glitches or Exploits: Using game-breaking glitches to duplicate items or exploit the game mechanics is forbidden. Play fair!

5. PvP is On: Feel free to engage in PvP, but make sure it’s consensual or within the context of raiding. Targeting new players excessively or camping at spawn to kill players repeatedly is not allowed.

6. Respect Staff and Other Players: Our staff team works hard to keep the server fair and enjoyable. If you have an issue, please bring it to their attention respectfully.

7. No Advertising: Do not advertise other Minecraft servers, websites, or anything unrelated to MysticTwinSMP without permission.

8. No Spamming or Excessive Caps: Keep chat clean and organized. No spamming, excessive caps, or flooding the chat with unnecessary messages.

9. If something seems like it might be against the spirit of fair play, it probably is. When in doubt, ask a staff member.

10. If you come across bugs or rule violations, report them to staff, but avoid calling players out in public chat.
